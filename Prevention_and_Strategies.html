Prevention and Strategies

Strong Passwords

Creating a strong password helps to ensure you are being protected from any incoming cyber attacks. The more complex your password, the harder it becomes for online predators to gain access to your information. Always remember to use a combination of upper and lowercase letters, numbers, special characters, and at least 8 characters in length.

Best Practices

  • Keeping devices up to date on software updates
  • Use Firewalls if you feel like you are potentially in harm's way
  • Never share personal information on the internet
