What is a cyber attack?

A cyber attack is a form of attack when a hacker tries to steal, destroy, or damage a network or system with malicious intent. It is important to keep yourself and others safe from any harm that a cyber attack can bring.

Types of Malware

A malware attack is any software designed to cause harm. This includes spams, bugs, worms, viruses, and more. Malware attacks are one of the commonly known attacks.

Ransom Attack

A ransom attack is when a hacker steals personal information and blocks access to their information until they pay to get their information back.

MITM Attack

A MITM attack, also known as a "Man in the middle" attack, is when a hacker puts themselves into the middle of two parties to intercept their communication and data without their knowledge.
